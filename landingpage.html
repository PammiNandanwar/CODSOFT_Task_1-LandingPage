Macarons are a delicate and visually appealing dessert that originated in France. They consist of two small, round, almond-based cookies sandwiched together with a creamy filling.

The best cookies have layers of texture. A slightly crisp outer shell that holds up to some heat with an inner core that's soft and chewy...
